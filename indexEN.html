INSTRUCTION

POEMIX (Poem) is created for better memorization of poems.

Navigation

In the navigation, you can increase the font (A), change the theme, view your statistics, or switch to the English version of the site.

Required fields

In the "Poem" field, paste the copied poem from the internet.

Optional fields

"Title and author of the poem" - enter the title and author of the poem (for yourself).

"Difficulty" - choose how many words you will see in the hint.

"Start from which line" - choose which line of the poem to start from, so you don't have to start from the first one.

"Timer" - set a goal to complete a poem in a certain amount of time.

Sequential mode

Sequential mode is where you need to establish the correct sequence of the lines of the poem. In this mode, it is better to remember the chronology of the poem, which is also a very important part.

In the line count field, choose the number of options you want to see in this mode.

Mosaic mode

Mosaic mode (puzzle) is the same as sequential mode, but in sequential mode you specify the chronology of the lines, while in mosaic mode you specify the order of the words in the line.

History

In the history, you can view all poems (their titles, if specified) as well as the time when you studied this poem.

There is also a very cool feature - copying. It allows you to click the required button to open the form already with the poem text.

Other details

You can find a hint by clicking the question mark when giving an answer.

We also have an ignore feature for special characters, so it's not necessary to put commas and periods when writing your answer.
