POEMIX

POEMIX (ukranian "Віршич") was created to help better memorize poems

In the first field, enter the title of the poem and the author (optional)

In the "Poem" field, paste the copied poem from the internet

The "Difficulty" field allows you to select how many words will appear in the hint. (Optional)

You can find the hint by clicking on the question mark when answering

"Starting line" - choose which line in the poem to start from. (Optional)

P.S. Special characters are ignored, so it's not necessary to use commas and periods when typing your answer.

You can also adjust the font size by clicking on the "A"
